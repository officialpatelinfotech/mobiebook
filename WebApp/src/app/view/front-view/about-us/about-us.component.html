About Us

We are a young team of IT professionals dedicated to the photo print industry. Our love affair with the photo industry began in 2005 & since then the industry has been kind enough to guide us & help us grow. Mobiebook is not a replacement for a printed photo book or to compete with printers, but we are here to complement them.

We truly believe Memories are best enjoyed when they are printed. In fact I printed 4 wedding albums for myself and that too from different countries - Australia & USA. We want to empower the professional photographer with tools which will help in earn more business & get maximum recognition for each photo they click or for the long hours they put on editing.

Photography connects us all & we would like to do our best to help it grow more. We hope you love our solutions & together we can take the Photo Industry to greater heights.
